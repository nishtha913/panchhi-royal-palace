ABOUT US

Inspired by majestic palace-style architecture, our venue reflects elegance, luxury, and grandeur at every corner. From the magnificent entrance to the beautifully designed interiors, Panchhi Royal Palace offers a regal atmosphere that elevates weddings, receptions, and grand celebrations.

With the capacity to accommodate 500 to 2000 guests comfortably, our venue features both spacious indoor banquet halls and beautifully maintained outdoor lawns.

Conveniently located near Chamaria Road, Rohtak, with dedicated parking space for over 200 vehicles, we ensure your guests experience both comfort and convenience.
